# Code of Conduct

ThreatMapper follows a simple professional conduct standard.

Participants should:

- Be respectful and specific.
- Critique behavior, evidence, code, and documentation rather than people.
- Keep discussions focused on CTI, detection engineering, deployment, and project quality.
- Avoid posting private data, exploit instructions for unauthorized systems, credentials, or victim-sensitive material.

Maintainers may close or remove comments that are abusive, off-topic, spam, or unsafe.
